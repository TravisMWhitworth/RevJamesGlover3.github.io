Rev. James H Glover III is a 30-year-old native of Glasgow, KY. At the age of 5 Rev. Glover lll accepted the Lord as his savior, then at the age of 17 he acknowledged the call on his life to preach and spread the gospel. After much prayer, October 12th, 2008 Rev. Glover lll has a heart for God and has served in various areas of ministry.
